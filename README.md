Zomato Restaurants Analysis (Group Project)

Project Overview

This is a collaborative data analytics project based on the Zomato Restaurants Dataset.
Our goal was to study restaurant patterns, customer preferences, and business insights that can influence decision-making in the food delivery industry.



Objectives

Understand the structure and characteristics of restaurant listings
Analyse cost patterns across cuisines and locations
Identify factors that impact restaurant ratings
Explore price range distribution and online delivery trends
Generate insights for restaurant businesses and food platforms



Tools & Technologies

SQL — Data querying & cleaning
Excel — Data exploration & summary
Tableau / Power BI — Dashboard & visualization



Project Structure

zomato-restaurants-analysis
Dashboards (Tableau/Power BI visuals)
SQL Queries / Scripts
Excel exploration file



Key Insights

Restaurants with higher ratings tend to charge relatively higher average prices
Popular cuisines like North Indian & Chinese dominate listed restaurants
Online delivery is more common in mid-priced restaurants
Average cost for two varies significantly by city and restaurant type
Customer ratings do not always correlate with number of votes
